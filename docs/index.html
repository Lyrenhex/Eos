Support at your fingertips

Mental health services are declining, with budget cuts to national healthcare schemes; nonexistant mental health schemes; and a lack of understanding or education on the topic of mental health. Nowadays, it's frequent for people to simply tell you to "ring a hotline", and think their job is done... But what about those who can't ring a hotline? For fear of identification, of being seen as weak, or another multitude of reasons?

It is for this reason that Eos was created: a modern, innovative solution to a rising crisis. The Eos app offers: mood tracking, to help identify trends and patterns (such as those prevalent within seasonally-affective disorders (SADs)); carefully considered, dynamic responses based on reported moods to provide resources when in low mood states; and one-on-one anonymous chats with another app user, allowing you to help them, to help yourself (and vice versa).

For peace of mind, Eos is completely open-source, free software. We operate under a strict confidentiality agreement. Why not take Eos for a spin, see how it works?
